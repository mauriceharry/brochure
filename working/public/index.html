The club is a privileged circle of amateur wine lovers, curious and eager to discover new flavours and, occasionally, exclusive cuvées.

Each month, we carefully curate a collection of wines packaged in a convenient box, which we can deliver directly to your doorstep or to a location of your preference.

The club represents an immersion in the wines of the various regions of the south of France and we invite you to discover their cuvées and their vineyards.

Our winemakers are, like us, passionate, self-demanding, lovers of the land that they know so well, thanks to many years, sometimes centuries of experience.

We’re available and ready to answer your questions and respond to your specific needs. We are also open to providing wine for your events such as family reunions, weddings, and more.

From the tiny village in the south of France where our friendship was born, we have decided to help you truly discover the wines and the region

Greg, the Englishman and technologist, has lived in the south of France for many years. Over time he's discovered the wines and the rich character of the region.

Laurent, the Frenchman and sommelier, is from the Uzège and his work is literally in wine. With decades of experience in hospitality, the restaurant trade and, later, as a specialist wine merchant, he loves to share his passion for wine.
